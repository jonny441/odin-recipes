Honey Garlic Pork Chops

In a sunny summer day nothing can hit better than a Honey Garlic Pork Chop.

Having a cookout what better food to have other than Pork chops.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup ketchup
  • 2 2/3 tablespoons honey
  • 2 tablespoons low-sodium soy sauce
  • 2 cloves garlic, crushed
  • 6 (4 ounce) ( 1-inch thick) pork chops

Directions

  1. Preheat grill for medium heat, lightly oil the grate.
  2. Add ketchup, honey, soy sauce, and garlic together in a bowl and whisk
  3. Burn the pork chops on both sides on the preheated barbecue. Delicately brush coat onto each side of the chops as they cook; flame broil until not pink within the center, approximately 7 to 9 minutes per side. An instant-read thermometer embedded into the center should read 145 degrees F (63 degrees C).
