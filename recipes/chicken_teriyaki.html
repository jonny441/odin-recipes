Grilled Chicken Teriyaki Skewers with Miso Ranch

Grilled Chicken Teriyaki skewers just like or slighty better than the China Buffet Restaurant.

A good meal to make while having a cookout on a sunny breezy day.

Ingredients

Chicken Skewers

  • 12 bamboo skewers
  • 1/2 cup soy sauce
  • 1/2 cup sake
  • 1/3 cup mirin (Japanese sweet wine)
  • 1/4 cup brown sugar
  • 2 tablespoons minced green onions
  • 2 teaspoons finely grated ginger
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil

Miso Ranch Dressing

  • 3/4 cup mayonnaise
  • 1/3 cup buttermilk
  • 1/4 cup sour cream
  • 1 tablespoon minced greed onion
  • 1 tablespoon white miso paste, or to taste
  • 1 clove garlic, crushed
  • 1 tablespoon finely chopped fresh tarragon
  • 1 tablespoon finely chopped fresh dill
  • freshly ground black pepper to taste
  • 1 pinch cayenne pepper

Directions

  1. Soak bamboo skewers in water.
  2. Cut chicken lengthwise, the cut to reasonable size (for skewer).
  3. Place chicken in bowl. Pour in soy sauce, sake, mirin, brown sugar, green onions, ginger, and oil.
  4. Toss by hand until well combined. Cover in plastic wrap and marinate in refrigerator for 2 to 6 hours.
  5. Make the dressing. Combine mayonnaise, buttermilk, sour cream, and miso in a bowl, add green onion, garlic, tarragon, dill, and chives. Season with black pepper and cayenne. Whisk dressing until well combined.
  6. Thread chicken pieces onto skewers. Strain marinade into saucepan, bring to boil to make the glaze.
  7. preheat the grill for medium-high heat. Grill skewers, basting with the reserved marinade. Until meat firms up.
  8. Let sit for 4 to 5 min and enjoy.
