Fluffy Microwave Scrambled Eggs

Like scrambled eggs, can't use the stove and you are hungry, well here is a recipe for you.

Ingredients

  • 4 eggs
  • 1/4 cup milk
  • 1/8 teaspoon salt

Directions

  1. Crack the eggs into a microwave safe bowl.
  2. Add milk and salt, mix well.
  3. Put bowl into microwave for 30 sec, take bowl out, whisk well, return into microwave for 30 more seconds.
  4. Repeat every 30 seconds for 2 minutes. Stop when eggs are as you desire.
